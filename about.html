I am currently a graduate student studying cartography and GIS through UW-Madison. I previously got my undergraduate degree from Scripps College in Claremont, CA in Environmental Analysis. My favorite part about GIS and mapmaking is the intersection of art and science/logic.

When not studying or doing homework, I love spending time in nature, especially in the woods or by a lake. I adore animals and am hoping to adopt a cat soon! I enjoy reading, baking and cooking, sewing, playing video games, and spending time with family and friends. I love taking photos, especially with film cameras, and minored in Studio Art with a photography concentration in my undergrad studies.
