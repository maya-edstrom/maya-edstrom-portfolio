FOODSHED PROJECT

This was a project I completed in my ungergraduate studies at Scripps College for an art-focused Environmental Analysis class called "Visual Ecology." I found an old map in my basemap and went through all food items in my pantry and fridge, figuring out where each one was sourced to the best of my ability. I then drew lines from where I live to these locations, showing how huge our foodsheds have become due to globalization, American imperialism and resource extractionalism.
